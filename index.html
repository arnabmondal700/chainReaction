How to Play

  1. Take turns placing an orb in any empty cell, or any cell you already own.
  2. Each cell can hold up to its capacity — 2 orbs in a corner, 3 on an edge, 4 in the interior.
  3. Reach capacity and the cell explodes, sending one orb to each neighboring cell and capturing them.
  4. Captured cells can explode too, chaining across the board.
  5. Own every orb on the board to win.

Strategy Tips

  • Corners and edges explode fast (2–3 orbs) — cheap but exposed.
  • Interior cells hold more orbs and can trigger the biggest chains.
  • Cells one orb from exploding pulse gently — treat them as live threats.
  • Don't stack orbs next to a strong opponent cluster; one move can flip it all.
